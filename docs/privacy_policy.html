Waker – Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

1. Overview

Waker is a simple keep-alive app that periodically sends HTTP GET requests to URLs you provide, so that remote servers stay awake. We take your privacy seriously — Waker is designed to collect as little data as possible.

2. Data we collect

Waker collects no personal data. The only information stored by the app is what you explicitly enter:

  • Ping event name — a human-readable label you choose
  • Target URL — the URL to ping (chosen by you)
  • Interval settings — min/max minutes between pings
  • Enabled/disabled state of each ping event

This data is stored only on your device using Android SharedPreferences. It is never uploaded to any server, never shared with any third party, and never leaves your device unless you use Android's standard backup/restore feature.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or crash reports
  • No advertising identifiers

4. Network requests

Waker sends HTTP GET requests to the URLs you configure. These requests are made on your behalf and solely to the servers you specify. Waker itself has no back-end server, no analytics endpoint, and receives no data from these requests other than an HTTP response code shown on screen.

5. Permissions used

  • INTERNET — to send the keep-alive pings you configure.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC — to run the ping loop reliably in the background on Android 14+.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to restart active ping events automatically after a device reboot.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — to request that Android does not kill the foreground service while the screen is off, ensuring your configured ping intervals are met.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to display a persistent status notification (required on Android 13+) so you can see the service is running.

6. Third-party services

Waker does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services.

7. Data retention

Your ping event data stays on your device until you delete individual events inside the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling removes all data.

8. Children's privacy

Waker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Because the app itself does not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository: https://github.com/pritam-ray/waker
